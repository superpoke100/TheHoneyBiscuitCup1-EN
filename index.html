Manual

1. Player 1 and Player 2 can be toggled at the top.

2. Left Click an Operator to purchase them. Right Click if they are purchased for free.

3. The final score is computed based on the rule book.

4. Enter your in-game score in the in-game score box.

5. In Item 1, Enter the number of relics obtained.

6. In Item 2, select the boss icon to compute the score. Bonuses for Flawless Clear and additional relics can be applied by selecting them.

7. The order of the boss icons in Item 2 is the same as the rulebook. The normal map ("Defining the World") has a purple icon, and the modified map ("Stasis") has a red icon.

8. In Item 3, check ALL conditions that have been satisfied.

9. In Item 4, select your starting squad.

10. In Item 5, select all operators that were recruited without being unlocked. The score for Player 2 includes the bonus for unused coins.

11. Items 6 and 7 should be modified based on your situation.
